Delta Zeta Kappa

Sisterhood.Academics.Service. Since 2004.

Delta Zeta Kappa was founded on October 25th, 2004 by fifteen ladies that sought out a unified and constant sisterhood that would last beyond college years.

A sisterhood like no other, Delta Zeta Kappa strives to enhance the college experience by creating strong bonds with women from campuses all over Southern California. Sisters of Delta Zeta Kappa pride themselves in working hard to excel in all their endeavors and to make a difference in their campuses, communities, work places, and each others lives.
